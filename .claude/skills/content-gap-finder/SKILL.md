# Content Gap Finder

## Context

ClearLegalTips has 50 published articles covering:
- Legal document templates (IDs 131–150): NDA, LLC agreements, lease agreements, etc.
- Cost/calculator guides (IDs 151–165): LLC costs, attorney fees, filing fees by state
- How-to guides (IDs 166–175): Form LLC online, register a business, file a will
- State/specialty guides (IDs 176–180): State-specific LLC, real estate, estate planning

**Target affiliate programs:** LawDepot, ZenBusiness, Northwest Registered Agent, Rocket Lawyer, LegalZoom, Nolo, Incfile

**Primary competitors:** Nolo.com, LegalZoom Blog, UpCounsel Blog, Rocket Lawyer Blog, Avvo

## Instructions

### Step 1 — Define Scope

Ask (or infer from user request):
- Topic area: business formation | contracts | estate planning | real estate | family law | all
- Competitor to analyze: specific site or general gap analysis
- Priority: high search volume OR high affiliate revenue potential OR both

### Step 2 — Gap Analysis Framework

For each competitor topic cluster, evaluate:

| Criteria | Questions |
|---|---|
| **Covered** | Does ClearLegalTips have an article matching this intent? |
| **Affiliate fit** | Does the topic naturally lead to a product recommendation? |
| **Search intent** | Is it informational → transactional (best for us)? |
| **State specificity** | Can we expand a general article to 5–10 state variants? |
| **Difficulty** | Is this a topic a general audience can DIY without a lawyer? |

### Step 3 — Topic Categories to Always Check

**High-value gaps to look for:**
1. **State-specific LLC guides** — "How to Form an LLC in [State]" × 50 states (we have 176-180, very limited)
2. **Comparison articles** — "ZenBusiness vs Northwest Registered Agent" (affiliate goldmine)
3. **Template variants** — "Simple LLC Operating Agreement Template" vs "Multi-Member LLC Operating Agreement"
4. **Cost breakdowns** — "How Much Does a Will Cost?" "Probate Lawyer Fees by State"
5. **Annual tasks** — "LLC Annual Report Requirements by State" "How to Renew a Business License"
6. **DIY legal checklists** — "Small Business Legal Checklist" "New LLC Compliance Checklist"

### Step 4 — Output Format

Produce a prioritized gap list:

```
CONTENT GAP ANALYSIS — [Date]
Competitor Analyzed: [site]
Topic Area: [area]

TIER 1 — HIGH PRIORITY (write in next 30 days)
1. [Topic] | Est. Volume: [X]/mo | Affiliate: [service] | Reason: [why high priority]

TIER 2 — MEDIUM PRIORITY (write in next 60 days)
...

TIER 3 — OPPORTUNISTIC (write when resources allow)
...

STATE EXPANSION OPPORTUNITIES
Articles that could be forked into state-specific variants:
- [Existing article] → [State list]

COMPARISON ARTICLE OPPORTUNITIES
- [Service A] vs [Service B] — Target keyword: [keyword]
```

## Examples

**Input:** "Find content gaps vs Nolo.com in business formation"
**Output:** Tier 1 includes "How to Form a Single-Member LLC" (10K/mo, high affiliate fit for ZenBusiness), "LLC vs S-Corp: What's the Difference" (8K/mo), state-specific LLC guides for top 10 states by population.
