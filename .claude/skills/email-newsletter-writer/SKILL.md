# Email Newsletter Writer

## Context

ClearLegalTips email newsletter converts subscribers into affiliate revenue through:
1. Driving traffic back to high-converting articles
2. Direct affiliate promotions with context
3. Building trust over time (readers don't buy on first visit)

**Newsletter frequency:** Monthly  
**Tone:** Same as site — clear, professional, empathetic. No hard sell.  
**Goal:** 1 affiliate click per 50 subscribers per send = realistic early target

## Newsletter Structure

```
Subject Line (max 50 chars): [curiosity + benefit]
Preview Text (max 90 chars): [expands on subject, creates open]

---

[HEADER: ClearLegalTips Monthly Legal Tips]

Hi [First Name],

[Opening hook — 2 sentences. Something relevant happening in legal/business world, or a reader question.]

THIS MONTH'S LEGAL TIPS:

1. [Tip 1 — 3-4 sentences. Links to an article.]
2. [Tip 2 — 3-4 sentences. Links to an article.]
3. [Tip 3 — 3-4 sentences. Links to an article.]

[FEATURED RESOURCE BOX]
This month we're featuring: [service name]
[2-3 sentences explaining why and for whom]
[CTA button: "Check it out →" → affiliate link]

QUICK LEGAL REMINDER:
[1 seasonal or timely legal reminder — LLC annual reports due, tax implications, etc.]

Until next month,
[Author name]
ClearLegalTips

[FOOTER: Unsubscribe | Privacy Policy | ClearLegalTips.com]
---
```

## Subject Line Formulas

| Formula | Example |
|---|---|
| Question | "Do you need an operating agreement?" |
| Number + benefit | "3 things every LLC owner should do in January" |
| Warning | "This LLC mistake costs $500+ to fix" |
| New content | "New guide: LLC vs S-Corp (which saves more in taxes?)" |
| Seasonal | "Annual report season: is yours due soon?" |

## Affiliate Promotion Guidelines

- **One primary affiliate per newsletter** — don't promote multiple services in one email
- **Always give context** — "If you're forming an LLC this month, ZenBusiness currently has a $49 plan that includes..."
- **Don't hard-sell** — "We use and recommend" framing, not "CLICK HERE NOW"
- **Disclosure required** — include in footer: "This email contains affiliate links. We may earn a commission."
- **Match promo to season**: January (new year, new LLC), March-April (tax season), September (Q4 business planning)
