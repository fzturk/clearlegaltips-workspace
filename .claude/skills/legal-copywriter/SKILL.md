# Legal Copywriter

## Context

Legal affiliate content has a unique copywriting challenge: the reader is skeptical (legal = complicated/expensive), cautious (don't want to make a mistake), and DIY-motivated (wants to handle it themselves). Copy must simultaneously:
1. Reassure ("this is simpler than you think")
2. Inform ("here's exactly what you need")
3. Convert ("here's the best tool to do it")

Without crossing into legal advice or fear-mongering.

## Core Copywriting Principles for Legal Content

### 1. Specificity Over Vagueness
❌ "Form your LLC easily"  
✅ "Form your LLC in 3 steps — takes about 30 minutes online"

❌ "Save money on legal fees"  
✅ "The average attorney charges $800–$1,500 to form an LLC. Online services: $49–$299."

### 2. Empathy Before Pitch
Lead with acknowledgment of the reader's situation before the solution:
"Forming an LLC for the first time can feel overwhelming — especially with different rules for every state. Here's a clear path."

### 3. Risk Reversal
Remove the reader's fear of making a mistake:
"ZenBusiness has processed 700,000+ LLC formations. If something's missing from your filing, their support team catches it."

### 4. Outcome-First CTAs
❌ "Click here" / "Learn more" / "Get started"  
✅ "Form My LLC for $49" / "Get My Free NDA Template" / "See [State] LLC Requirements"

## Copy Types & Formulas

### Headlines (H1 / article titles)
**Formula 1 — Specific outcome:** "[Number] [Specific result] in [timeframe]"
"Form an LLC in Texas in 3 Steps (Done in 1 Business Day)"

**Formula 2 — Cost anchor:** "How Much Does [X] Cost? ([Year] Complete Breakdown)"
"How Much Does an LLC Cost? 2025 State-by-State Guide"

**Formula 3 — Question + implied answer:** "Do You Need [X]? (Honest Answer)"
"Do You Need an Operating Agreement? (Honest Answer)"

**Formula 4 — Comparison:** "[A] vs [B]: Which is Right for You?"
"ZenBusiness vs Northwest Registered Agent: 2025 Comparison"

### CTA Button Copy
| Context | CTA Text |
|---|---|
| After explaining a service | "Start with [Service] →" |
| After showing a template | "Get Your Free [Document] Template →" |
| After cost comparison | "Form My LLC for $49 →" |
| After how-to guide | "Do It With [Service] →" |
| After state guide | "Form My [State] LLC →" |
| Lead magnet | "Get My Personalized Recommendation →" |

### Email Subject Lines
**Formula 1 — Specific number:** "The 3 LLC mistakes costing small business owners $1,000+"
**Formula 2 — Question:** "Does your LLC have an operating agreement?"
**Formula 3 — State-specific:** "Texas LLC owners: your annual report may be due"
**Formula 4 — Time-sensitive:** "New: 2025 LLC formation costs by state"

## Instructions

1. **Identify the copy type** (headline / CTA / email subject / comparison / landing page section)
2. **Identify the reader's current state** (just read about topic X, at decision point, skeptical)
3. **Apply the appropriate formula**
4. **Write 2–3 variants** for testing
5. **Check against brand voice rules** (no legal advice absolutes, no fear-mongering, no "simply"/"just")
