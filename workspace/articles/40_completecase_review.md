## How CompleteCase Works

### The Process (4 Steps)

**1. Complete the questionnaire (30-60 minutes)**
Answer questions about your marriage, property, debts, children, and agreed-upon terms. The questionnaire adapts based on your state's specific requirements.

**2. Documents generated (1-2 business days)**
CompleteCase generates your complete divorce paperwork — customized for your county courthouse.

**3. Review and sign**
You receive the documents, review everything, and sign where indicated. Your spouse signs acceptance of service (or you arrange formal service).

**4. File with the court**
Follow the included filing instructions to submit your documents to the court. CompleteCase does NOT file for you — you handle the actual filing.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is CompleteCase legitimate?
Yes — CompleteCase has operated since 2001, has prepared documents for hundreds of thousands of divorces, and is a legitimate document preparation service. They are NOT a law firm and don't provide legal advice.

### What's the court-acceptance guarantee?
If the court rejects your documents for a preparation error, CompleteCase revises and resubmits them at no extra cost. This doesn't cover rejections due to incomplete information YOU provided or contested issues.

### Can I get a refund?
CompleteCase offers refunds before document generation. After documents are generated, refund availability is limited. Review their refund policy before purchasing.

### Does CompleteCase work for divorces with children?
Yes — the service generates parenting plans, custody agreements, and child support worksheets for your state. However, both parents must agree on ALL custody terms.

### How long until my divorce is final?
CompleteCase prepares documents in 1-2 days. After filing, finalization depends on your state's processing time and mandatory waiting period (30 days to 6+ months).
