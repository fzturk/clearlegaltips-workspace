## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I be my own registered agent?
Yes — if you have a physical address in the state and are available during business hours. Your home address becomes public record.

### What happens if I don't have a registered agent?
The state can administratively dissolve your LLC. You also risk missing legal documents (lawsuits), which can result in default judgments against you.

### How quickly can I get a registered agent set up?
Instantly — most services provide your agent address immediately upon signup. You can use it on your LLC formation documents the same day.

### Can I change my registered agent later?
Yes — file a simple change form with your state ($0-$25 fee). New agent takes effect upon filing. Most services handle this paperwork for you.
