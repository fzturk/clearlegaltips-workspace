## 2. DEFINITIONS

**"Separate Property"** — Property owned by a Party before marriage, plus:
- Inheritances received by one Party (regardless of when received)
- Gifts received by one Party from third parties
- Income or appreciation on separate property (unless commingled)
- Property designated as separate in this Agreement

**"Marital Property"** — Property acquired by either Party during the marriage using marital funds, except as classified as Separate Property above.

**"Commingled Property"** — Separate property that has been mixed with marital property to the extent that it can no longer be traced or identified.
