## 8. SUBLESSOR'S RESPONSIBILITIES

Sublessor remains fully responsible to the Landlord under the Original Lease. Sublessor shall:
(a) Remain liable for all obligations under the Original Lease;
(b) Forward any landlord communications to Sublessee;
(c) Not interfere with Sublessee's quiet enjoyment;
(d) Return the security deposit per terms of Section 4.

---

## 9. EARLY TERMINATION

Either party may terminate with [30] days' written notice. Upon early termination, Sublessee shall vacate and Sublessor shall return the deposit less lawful deductions.

## 10. MOVE-OUT

Upon termination, Sublessee shall:
- Remove all personal belongings
- Return all keys and access devices
- Leave premises in same condition as received (normal wear excepted)
- Provide forwarding address
