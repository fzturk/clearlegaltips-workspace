## 9. NON-COMPETE

During the Venture term and for [6 / 12] months after, no Party shall engage in activities that directly compete with or undermine the Venture's purpose, without written consent of all other Parties.

---

## 10. LIABILITY

**10.1** Each Party shall be responsible for their own acts and omissions.

**10.2** Each Party shall indemnify the others against claims arising from their negligence or breach of this Agreement.

**10.3** No Party shall have authority to bind another Party to personal liability without express written authorization.

---

## 11. TERMINATION

**11.1 Termination Events:**
(a) Completion of the project purpose;
(b) Mutual written agreement;
(c) Expiration of the term;
(d) Material breach not cured within [30] days;
(e) Insolvency or bankruptcy of any Party.

**11.2 Effect of Termination:**
- Outstanding obligations shall be fulfilled
- Joint Venture assets liquidated and distributed per ownership %
- Debts paid before distributions
- Accounting within [60] days of termination
