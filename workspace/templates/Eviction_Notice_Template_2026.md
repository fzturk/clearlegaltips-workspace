## IMPORTANT STATE-SPECIFIC NOTES

- **California:** Different notice periods apply (3-day for rent, varies for other violations; 30/60-day depending on tenancy length). Rent control cities may have additional requirements.
- **New York:** 14-day notice for nonpayment; 30-day for month-to-month (outside NYC). NYC has strict just-cause requirements.
- **Texas:** 3-day notice to vacate (unless lease specifies otherwise). No cure period required by statute.
- **Florida:** 3-day for rent; 7-day for lease violations (non-curable in some cases).
- **Illinois:** 5-day notice for rent; 10-day for lease violations; 30-day for month-to-month termination.

**ALWAYS verify your state and local requirements before serving any eviction notice.**
